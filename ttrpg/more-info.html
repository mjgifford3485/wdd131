Resources

In order to play, you are going to need dice, books, and other players

Dice Roller

Dice are an essential part of any tabletop roleplaying game. Most people have a 6 sided die or two laying around, but tabletop roleplaying games require all different sizes. You can buy dice from any gaming store or online, but if you want to try out a game without spending money feel free to use the dice roller below. In the first box below, type in the number of dice you want to roll and in the second box the size of the dice.

Books

Physical copies of books can be founr online or in any gaming or bookstore. The best resource for purchasing digital books is drivethrurpg.com.

Other Players

Thanks to modern technology, it is possible to find games and play with others online using websites such as roll20.net or demiplane.com.
